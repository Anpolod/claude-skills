# Source whitelist

Prefer these outlets in the order listed within each beat. When `web_search`
returns a result from this list, pick it over an unfamiliar aggregator.

## Merchant shipping
- TradeWinds (tradewindsnews.com) — paywalled but strong rate/fixture coverage
- Lloyd's List (lloydslist.com) — paywalled, authoritative
- Splash247 (splash247.com) — free, fast, broad
- Seatrade Maritime (seatrade-maritime.com)
- Riviera Maritime Media (rivieramm.com)
- Hellenic Shipping News (hellenicshippingnews.com) — good aggregator
- gCaptain (gcaptain.com)
- Maritime Executive (maritime-executive.com)
- ShippingWatch (shippingwatch.com) — paywalled, Nordic focus
- Clarksons Research, Baltic Exchange, Drewry — for rate/index data

## Offshore
- Upstream (upstreamonline.com) — paywalled, authoritative on E&P
- Offshore Engineer (oedigital.com)
- Rigzone (rigzone.com)
- Offshore (offshore-mag.com)
- WindEurope (windeurope.org) — offshore wind policy
- 4C Offshore (4coffshore.com) — offshore wind projects database
- ReCharge News (rechargenews.com) — renewables

## Maritime technology
- The Motorship (motorship.com) — propulsion and machinery
- Ship & Bunker (shipandbunker.com) — fuels and bunkering
- Riviera Maritime Media — has strong tech vertical
- DNV, Lloyd's Register, ABS — class society press releases for new rules
- IMO (imo.org) — regulatory milestones
- Baird Maritime (bairdmaritime.com)

## Crewing & seafarer labor
- Seafarers Rights International (seafarersrights.org)
- ITF (itfseafarers.org) — global union
- Nautilus International (nautilusint.org) — officer union
- BIMCO (bimco.org) — employer perspective + workforce reports
- ICS (ics-shipping.org)
- Ship Universe, Marine Link — for manning agency / wage surveys

## Geopolitics & security
- Reuters (reuters.com)
- Bloomberg (bloomberg.com) — paywalled
- UKMTO (ukmto.org) — Red Sea / Gulf advisories
- EUNAVFOR (eunavfor.eu) — Operation Aspides updates
- Windward, Ambrey, Dryad Global — risk intelligence reports
- OFAC (treasury.gov/ofac) — sanctions designations
- S&P Global Commodity Insights — sanctions impact on flows

## Ports & logistics
- JOC (joc.com) — now part of S&P, paywalled
- Container News (container-news.com)
- World Cargo News (worldcargonews.com)
- Port Technology (porttechnology.org)
- Alphaliner — liner capacity data
- Xeneta — container rate data
- Panama Canal Authority, Suez Canal Authority — official transit stats

## Multi-language sources (read, translate in blurb)
- Sudostroenie.info (RU) — Russian shipbuilding
- PortNews (RU/EN) — Russian-focused
- Xinhua Maritime (CN/EN)
- Nikkei Asia (EN) — Japanese and Korean yards, K-Line / MOL / NYK
- Tradewinds Korea section
- Ukrainian: Center for Transport Strategies (cts.dp.ua)

## Sources to avoid
- Content farms recycling press releases without attribution
- LinkedIn posts — unless the poster is the named company making the
  announcement and there is no better source
- Opinion-only blogs without editorial standards
- Anything that a web search returns labeled as "sponsored" / "promoted"
